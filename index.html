Enjoy Your Stay
in Our Hotel

Welcome to the Hotel Aashirwad and Restaurant, located on the tranquil lakeside of Pokhara, offers a serene escape for travelers seeking a blend of natural beauty and modern comfort. Nestled amidst lush greenery, our hotel provides breathtaking views and a peaceful ambiance, making it the perfect destination for relaxation and rejuvenation. Guests can enjoy a variety of amenities and services, including comfortable accommodations, exquisite dining, and easy access to the city's main attractions.
